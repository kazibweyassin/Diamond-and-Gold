## Implementation Guide

### Targeting Parameters

**Primary Audience:**
- **Location:** Uganda, Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana (start here, expand to 15+ countries)
- **Age:** 20-45 (students, young professionals, business travelers)
- **Interests:** Travel, visas, study abroad, immigration, business travel, education
- **Behaviors:** Recently searched visa-related keywords, visited visa websites
- **Income:** Middle to upper-middle (can afford service fee)

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- Your WhatsApp contact list (if you have 500+ contacts)
- Website visitors (if you have 100+ visits/month)
- Email subscribers

### Budget Allocation (Monthly)

| Ad Set | Budget | Target Clicks | Target Leads | Target Conversions (20%) |
|--------|--------|---|---|---|
| Ad Set 1 (Pain Point) | $250 | 20-25 | 10-12 | 2-2.5 |
| Ad Set 2 (Social Proof) | $150 | 15-18 | 8-10 | 1.5-2 |
| Ad Set 3 (Urgency) | $100 | 12-15 | 6-8 | 1-1.5 |
| **TOTAL (Phase 1)** | **$500** | **50-60** | **25-30** | **5-6** |

**Expected Revenue:** $750-900/month (at $150-200 per conversion)

### Testing Timeline

**Week 1-2: Setup**
- Create ads in Facebook Ads Manager
- Set up conversion tracking (form submissions, WhatsApp clicks)
- Configure audience targeting
- A/B test creative variations (headlines, images, CTA copy)

**Week 3-4: Launch & Monitor**
- Launch all 6 ad sets simultaneously (with equal budget split)
- Daily monitoring of CTR, CPC, CPL (cost per lead)
- Kill underperforming ads by Day 7 (CTR < 1% = pause)
- Double budget on top 2-3 performers

**Month 2: Scale**
- Increase budget 2-3x on winning ads
- Add retargeting audiences (website visitors, form starts)
- Test new creative variations based on winner insights
- Expand to additional countries (test Kenya, Nigeria)

### Optimization Rules

**Pause ads if:**
- CTR < 0.8% (not compelling)
- CPC > $1.50 (too expensive)
- Conversion rate < 5% (sales funnel problem)

**Scale ads if:**
- CTR > 1.5% (very compelling)
- CPL < $20 (efficient)
- Conversion rate > 15% (strong funnel)

These ads should link to:

**Daily Monitoring:**
- Click-through rate (CTR) — Target: > 1%

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't promise visa approval** — Say "98% document-ready approval" instead  
❌ **Don't use generic stock photos** — Use real faces, real testimonials  
❌ **Don't link to homepage** — Link to application form or visa checker  
❌ **Don't set it and forget it** — Monitor daily, optimize weekly  
❌ **Don't test only 1 ad** — Test 3-5 variations per week  
❌ **Don't target too broad** — Be specific (Uganda ages 20-35, interests: visa)
