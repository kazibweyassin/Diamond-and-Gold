1. Executive Summary
This report documents findings from a full review of the Diamond Capital Africa website (diamondcapitalafrica.com) conducted in May 2026. The review assessed the site against international gold trading buyer expectations, competitor benchmarking (HP3 Global Limited), and general website credibility standards.
The site performs well in design, pricing transparency, and compliance positioning. However, several specific issues — some critical — must be addressed before the site can be considered fully effective for converting serious international buyers.
This report is structured by priority. Developers should address Critical issues first, followed by High, then Medium improvements.

3. Detailed Findings & Developer Instructions
3.1  FAQ Page — Incomplete Answers (CRITICAL)
URL: diamondcapitalafrica.com/faq
What the developer sees
The FAQ page currently lists 6 questions. Only the first question has a written answer. The remaining 5 questions render as empty accordion items with no content.

Required Action
Write and publish complete answers for all 5 unanswered questions. Suggested answers are provided below for each:

Q: How does Diamond Capital Africa ensure secure gold transactions?
Suggested answer: Every transaction follows a documented workflow. Buyers complete KYC verification before any funds move. Gold is independently assayed at ISO-certified labs before packing. Shipments travel with full-value insurance and real-time tracking. Your account manager is reachable throughout — from first quote to confirmed delivery.

Q: Who can trade gold with Diamond Capital Africa?
Suggested answer: We work with licensed refineries, institutional buyers, wealth managers, and registered trading companies. All buyers must complete our KYC process before their first order. We do not transact with unverified individuals or anonymous parties.

Q: What documentation is required for exporting gold from Uganda?
Suggested answer: Exports require a Uganda Minerals & Mining export permit, origin certificate, independent assay report, packing list and commercial invoice, and customs clearance documentation. We prepare and coordinate all of this on your behalf. Buyers need to provide company registration, beneficial ownership declaration, and banking details.

Q: Does Diamond Capital Africa work directly with local miners?
Suggested answer: Yes. We have verified partnerships with licensed artisanal miners across Uganda and Congo. We physically visit our partner mines before buying from them and document every source. We do not purchase from unverified or undocumented sources.

Q: What measures does Diamond Capital Africa take to prevent gold fraud?
Suggested answer: All transactions are confirmed directly through our Kampala office. We do not use third-party mandates or intermediaries without written authorisation. Always verify you are dealing with us through our official channels: info@diamondcapitalafrica.com or +256 (0) 704 833 021. Any offer that cannot be confirmed through these contacts should be treated as fraudulent.


3.2  Stock Photography on About Page (CRITICAL)
URL: diamondcapitalafrica.com/about — Field Gallery section
What the developer sees
The field gallery uses images sourced from Unsplash and Pexels. The image URLs are visible in page source. International buyers conducting due diligence may recognise these as stock photos, undermining the company's credibility claim of field verification and direct miner relationships.

Required Action
•	Replace all three gallery images with genuine operational photos from DCA's actual operations.
•	Suitable subjects: Kampala office interior or exterior, team members at work, gold lots being weighed or assayed, field visits to partner mines, packing and logistics operations.
•	Minimum recommended: 3–5 original photos. JPEG, minimum 800px wide, optimised for web.
•	Update each image's alt text and caption to reflect what is specifically shown.
•	If professional photography is not immediately available, even high-quality smartphone photos of real operations are preferable to stock imagery.

3.5  Fraud Warning Missing (HIGH)
URL: diamondcapitalafrica.com (homepage, footer)

Required Action
Add a visible fraud/impersonation notice to the website. This is especially important for Uganda-based gold companies given the frequency of scams in this sector. Recommended placement: homepage (below the hero or in the footer), and FAQ page.

Suggested notice copy
Diamond Capital Africa has become aware of fraudulent parties impersonating our company in gold transactions. To protect yourself:
•	We do not use third-party mandates or intermediaries without direct written authorisation from our Kampala office.
•	Always verify through our official contacts: info@diamondcapitalafrica.com / +256 (0) 704 833 021.
•	Diamond Capital Africa accepts no responsibility for losses arising from dealing with unauthorised individuals.
If in doubt — stop and verify directly.
